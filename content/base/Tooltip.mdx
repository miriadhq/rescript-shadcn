## Usage

```res showLineNumbers
<Tooltip>
  <Tooltip.Trigger render={<Button variant=Outline />}>
    {"Hover"->React.string}
  </Tooltip.Trigger>
  <Tooltip.Content>
    <p> {"Add to library"->React.string} </p>
  </Tooltip.Content>
</Tooltip>
```
