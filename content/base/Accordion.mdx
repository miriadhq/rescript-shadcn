## Usage

```res showLineNumbers
<Accordion defaultValue=["item-1"]>
  <Accordion.Item value="item-1">
    <Accordion.Trigger> {"Is it accessible?"->React.string} </Accordion.Trigger>
    <Accordion.Content>
      {"Yes. It adheres to the WAI-ARIA design pattern."->React.string}
    </Accordion.Content>
  </Accordion.Item>
</Accordion>
```
