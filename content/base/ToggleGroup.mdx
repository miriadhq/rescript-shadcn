## Usage

```res 
<ToggleGroup multiple=false>
  <ToggleGroup.Item value="a"> {"A"->React.string} </ToggleGroup.Item>
  <ToggleGroup.Item value="b"> {"B"->React.string} </ToggleGroup.Item>
  <ToggleGroup.Item value="c"> {"C"->React.string} </ToggleGroup.Item>
</ToggleGroup>
```
